# Polis
1-minute email news letter of how policies and bills impact individuals

# The Problem
When a bill or policy passes, the people who understand its implications are lawyers, lobbyists, and wealthy professionals with access to advisors. Everyone else finds out months later — through a higher tax bill, a changed insurance rate, or a new zoning law that affects their neighborhood. The knowledge gap isn't about intelligence. It's about access.

# The Solution
Polis is an email newsletter that gives every citizen — regardless of income, education, or language — an immediate, personalized answer to the only question that actually matters when judging a policy or bill: "What does this mean for me?"
